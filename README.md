Witness Protocol - Privacy & Security Documentation App

Witness Protocol is a mobile application designed to help individuals document and record instances of digital surveillance, harassment, and privacy violations in a secure, offline environment.

---

What Witness Protocol Does

Witness Protocol allows users to create a secure, chronological record of suspicious activities on their devices. All data is stored locally with strong encryption and does not require internet access, minimizing exposure to remote surveillance.

---

Why Witness Protocol?

In an age where digital privacy is increasingly fragile, individuals facing sophisticated surveillance often struggle to maintain reliable records of what is happening. Standard note-taking apps and cloud services can be accessed remotely, making it difficult to preserve evidence without interference.

Witness Protocol provides a simple, secure solution:

· Offline-first: Works without internet, keeping your records inaccessible to remote actors
· Local encryption: All data stays on your device, encrypted with strong security
· Visual evidence: Capture screenshots and photos directly within the app
· Chronological timeline: Organize events as they happen for clear documentation
· Reliable storage: Data remains intact even if other parts of your device are compromised

---

How It Works

1. Enable airplane mode on your device (recommended for maximum security)
2. Open Witness Protocol
3. Record the event: describe what happened, when it occurred, and attach any relevant visual evidence
4. Save securely - all data is encrypted locally

---

Who Is This For?

Witness Protocol is for individuals who:

· Suspect their communications are being intercepted
· Are targets of harassment or digital surveillance
· Need to preserve evidence for legal proceedings or advocacy work
· Want to maintain a personal record of events without relying on cloud services

Privacy Commitment

Witness Protocol does not:

· Send any data over the internet
· Store information in cloud services
· Share or access your personal information
· Use third-party analytics or tracking

Witness Protocol does:

· Store everything locally on your device
· Allow you full control over your records
· Provide you with a reliable, private documentation tool
